KEIREN STREET

HAIR

Keiren Street has established himself as one of the leading hair stylists in Australia, creating original looks for campaigns, editorial and shows and with an outstanding ability to translate international catwalk trends and apply them to a discerning local clientele.

Highly accomplished and with a longstanding tenure at Sydneys most acclaimed hair salon Valonz, he has confirmed his status as both an in-demand salon stylist and one of Australias top session stylists. His exceptional talent and down to earth personality also see him in constant demand with a long list of celebrity clients that include Toni Collette, Rose Byrne, Cate Blanchett, Asher Keddie, Elizabeth Debicki, Katherine Langford, Jennifer Hawkins to name a few.

Keiren is a regular contributor to Vogue, Marie Claire, Elle and Harpers Bazaar magazines and has an ever-expanding list of advertising clients. He is passionate about every aspect of hair styling - his keen eye ensures that whether its high fashion, beauty, catwalk, celebrity or advertising, the hair should speak for itself and not overwhelm. Yet it never goes unnoticed because great hair always forms an integral part of the overall image.

For the past ten years Keiren has travelled twice yearly to New York and Paris for international couture and ready to wear shows working alongside globally influential hair directors. In 2020 he lead the hair team for the Zimmerman London show at Annabels.

He is a major contributor to Australian Fashion Week, directing shows for Alice McCall, bassike, Toni Maticevski, The Iconic and others.

Keiren has a long standing tenure as a creative artist with WELLA Pro, including mentoring and educating up and coming talent and driving the brand association with Alice McCall.

As creative director of K-Set, Keiren has designed and developed a range of products for industry professionals.

Visionary, forward thinking and consistently producing work of the highest standard, Keirens ability and collaborative spirit are testament as to why he is recognised as one of Australias leading hair stylists.
